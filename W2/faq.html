Generic Website FAQs

Why make a generic website?

Generic websites don't offer much in terms of content, but they are a great way to show future employers that you know what you are doing.

Why name it 'Generic Website'?

There is no real unique features of this website, just like any 'Great Value' product. Why name the website anything else?
